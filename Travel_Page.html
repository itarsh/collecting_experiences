Are you looking for an adventure of a lifetime in Earth’s unexplored corners? Or perhaps a chance to find some me time away from the hustle and bustle of everyday life? Maybe you’d like to travel back in time and rediscover areas explored by great women of the past?It could be you’re raring to find an opportunity to experience in and education yourself on the what it’s like to be a female elsewhere?

Because you’re an empowered, ambitious and kick-ass woman, always looking for new opportunities for self-development, we’ve got some travel recommendations for you below. We have provided a range of experiences that will ensure your inner female superhero takes you to the places you deserve to go! Limit less, experience more.

#ACTION&ADVENTURE

Elle Voyage, Iceland

Iceland is a playground that promises you an incredible adventure with plenty of wow factor moments to be shared with your fellow voyageurs. From steaming mud pools and rejuvenating waters to breathtaking scenery, quirky bohemian towns with colourful buildings, mesmerising waterfalls and jagged icebergs.

Don't miss: The Northern light season from late August to mid-April!

Female Empowerment Retreat, Ibiza

Women are born to be sensitive and in touch with our natural deep wisdom. We have special softer strengths and qualities that need to be nurtured and nourished for us to really sit in our power, to be aligned with our true strength: our sacred feminine energy. You will discover your needs and learn how to nourish them, connect more deeply to your intuition, to your purpose, passions and path.

Don't miss: The turquoise waters of Benirras beach, just a 15-minute walk away!

Suffragette Women's Movement Walk, London

Discover what life was like for women at the dawn of the 20th century and how their position in society began to change. Follow in the footsteps of Emmeline Pankhurst, Mary ‘Slasher’ Richardson and the wave of Suffragettes who campaigned tirelessly for women’s rights. Remember the actions of ordinary women who lived their lives differently to the generations before them, shaping society through small acts of rebellion, such as joining the workforce. 

Don't Miss: Key locations including the Suffragette Fellowship Memorial and Parliament Square!

Suffragette Women's Movement Walk Website

Responsible Travel, Iran

As well as visiting some of Iran’s most celebrated sights, including the remains of ancient capital Persepolis, you will also be able to mix with local women in a truly authentic way. Be ready to hear from women of all ages, backgrounds and faiths as you visit a beauty salon, relax with a picnic in a women-only park where women can relax without a hijab, and use the women only carriages on the metro and train.

Don't miss: A rural homestay with the Qashqai people, meet some young female entrepreneurs, and learn about Iranian food!
